What is this?

Every year, NASAâ€™s Wisconsin Space Grant Consortium (WSGC) hosts the annual First Nations Launch (FNL) National Rocket Competition. This competition is an opportunity for students attending a Tribal College or University, or who are members of a campus AISES chapter, to design, build, and fly a rocket to be launched at a competition at the Richard Bong State Recreational Area in Kansasville, WI. The competition requires teams of undergraduate students to conceive, design, fabricate, and compete with high-powered rockets.
